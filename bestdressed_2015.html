Best Dressed NLP Researchers (2015)

Editor's (my) Notes
Some highlights from the past year:
  • I heard one last year's winner told his/her colleagues to stay away from me if they are not wearing nice clothes.
  • Another last year's winner told me that this list directs a big chunk of traffic to his homepage.
Another thought of the year:

  • Only a small fraction of women's clothes sold in the stores are great for CS academia, while most men's clothes are acceptable. I didn't realize this until this year, so maybe many people didn't think about this either. In the hotel elevator at EMNLP this year, I overheard a male student expressing his disapproval over a dress his group mate was wearing. Whether you are a man or a woman, I think this article "Coding Like a Girl" will worth a read.
Last year's list is here. If you want to nominate someone, please email me at xwe@cis.upenn.edu.

-- Wei Xu Dec 11, 2015
